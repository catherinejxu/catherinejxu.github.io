Welcome

Hi, I’m Catherine Xu. I am passionate about sustainability, entrepreneurship, and policy advocacy. My work spans multiple fields, from environmental initiatives to student empowerment and impactful public policy.

Today, I am a student at USC, double majoring in Business Administration (Finance) and Public Policy, with a minor in Computer Programming.

About Me

Born and raised in Thousand Oaks, California, I’ve always been deeply involved in my community. From playing varsity sports to leading policy initiatives, my journey has been one of learning, growth, and impact.

Professionally, I’ve worked in private equity, finance, and startups. As the founder of Optipin, I helped businesses identify optimal locations using ArcGIS, and as a summer analyst at Hunter Point Capital, I evaluated deals worth $730M+ in TEV.

I thrive on creating meaningful change and empowering others to use their voices effectively.

Projects

Over the years, I’ve led and contributed to a variety of projects:

  • Finance & Startups: Built investment theses for healthcare companies at Kinesis Growth Partners, and forecasted financial statements at Work From Home Studio.
  • Sustainability Education: Developed curriculums for climate action and environmental awareness in schools.
  • Menstrual Equity: Co-led the legislative strategy for California’s AB 367, ensuring free menstrual products in public schools and universities.

I continue to work on projects that bring together policy, technology, and community impact.
