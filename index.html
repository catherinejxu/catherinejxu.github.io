About Me

Hello! I'm Catherine Xu, an advocate for racial equity, environmental sustainability, and youth empowerment. I have a background in policy, youth organizing, and educational advocacy, with a focus on creating impactful change within communities.

I've worked with various youth-driven organizations, including the California Association of Student Councils, Diversify Our Narrative, and the Ventura County Youth Coalition, where I've led initiatives promoting equity in education, climate action, and community development.

Experience

  • Vice Chair - Student District Advisory Council - Representing students in district matters and contributing to policy on equity and school reopenings.
  • Leader - Ventura County Youth Coalition - Advocating for racial equity and environmental sustainability through youth-driven events and projects.
  • Policy Advocate - California Association of Student Councils - Led a $6.3 million budget request, contributed to state legislation, and championed racial equity in school curriculums.
  • Founder - Spark SC - Organizing entrepreneurship initiatives to promote social impact and empower young entrepreneurs.
